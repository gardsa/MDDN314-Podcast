Brian Eno

How to create 77 Million Paintings

Join Samantha Gard and James Erwin as they discuss the work and ideas of Brian Eno, a man known as the Godfather of ambient music. Eno has held many titles in the creative industry - composer, visual artist, and record producer to name a few. With a focus on his audio-visual installation 77 Million Paintings, Sam and James delve into the art of procedural generation, a number of Eno's personal philosophies, and the relevance of his work in contemporary media design. They also detail how his audio-visual projects have influenced their own design practice and ideologies, as well as how he paved the way for generative visual and sound design.

The podcast cover art was adapted from an image sourced from the book Brian Eno: Visual Music. The sound clips played throughout the podcast were sourced from Youtube and Vimeo, and are examples of Brian Eno's work on the Windows 95 startup sound, Music for Airports and 77 Million Paintings.
